Argentina

Argentina, land of "el mate", tasty roasts, tango, good football and more
It is the second largest country in South America, after Brazil. It is a federation of 23 provinces and an autonomous city, Buenos Aires, the most industrialized region. The country is rich in natural resources, particularly lead, zinc, tin, copper, magnesium, oil, and uranium. In Argentina, each of the twenty-three federated states named in the Constitution of the Argentine Nation is called a province, which together with the Autonomous City of Bueno Aires constitute the first-order jurisdictions-territorial divisions of the country.
